One unexpected phenomena that I found was how more casual members would use bike stations by the coast rather than stations that were more inland. This makes me believe there are more casual members that live by the coast, or there are attractions that may encourage people to use the stations for a short time. This assumes that casual members use the stations and don’t become members because they don’t use the stations quite often
